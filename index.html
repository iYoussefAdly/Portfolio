I'm Youssef Adly, a Flutter developer from Beni-Suef, Egypt, a fresh graduate of 2026 with a B.Sc. in Information Systems from the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, Beni-Suef University.

I learn fast, take feedback seriously, and I'm ready to start contributing from day one.

🌱 PlantLife — Smart IoT & AI Plant Care

Graduation Project

Role: Flutter & System Development

An Arduino & ESP32-CAM system that tracks plant vitals in real time — temperature, humidity, soil moisture, light — and uses AI to diagnose diseases from leaf photos. The app generates step-by-step treatment plans and tracks recovery week by week until full rehabilitation.

Why this project matters

I wanted to prove I can build beyond pure Flutter. PlantLife forced me to handle real hardware, live sensor data streams, and AI model output in one product — connecting the physical world to a mobile interface, end to end.
